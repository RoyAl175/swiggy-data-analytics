# Key Business Insights – Swiggy Order Analysis

## 1. Overall Performance
- The platform recorded approximately 197K orders, indicating strong customer demand.
- Total revenue is around ₹53 million, reflecting healthy monetization.
- The average dish price is about ₹268, showing that customers prefer mid-priced meals.
- The average rating is ~4.34, indicating high overall customer satisfaction.

## 2. Time-Based Trends
- Order volume remains consistent across months with noticeable peaks in January, April, July, and August.
- Quarterly analysis shows the highest order volume in Q2 (April–June).
- Weekend demand is significantly higher than weekdays, especially on Saturdays and Sundays.

## 3. Location Insights
- Bengaluru leads in total orders, followed by Mumbai and Hyderabad.
- A small number of major cities contribute a large share of total orders.
- Karnataka is the highest revenue-generating state, followed by Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Maharashtra.

## 4. Restaurant & Cuisine Performance
- Large restaurant chains such as KFC, McDonald’s, Pizza Hut, and Burger King dominate total revenue.
- The “Recommended” category has the highest order volume, highlighting the impact of platform recommendations.
- Some cuisines receive high ratings despite lower order volumes, indicating niche but high-quality offerings.

## 5. Pricing & Customer Behavior
- Most orders fall within the ₹100–199 and ₹200–299 price ranges.
- Orders priced above ₹500 are fewer but still contribute notable revenue.
- Customers appear price-sensitive while occasionally opting for premium items.

## 6. Quality Signals
- The majority of ratings fall between 4 and 5.
- Low-rated orders are minimal, indicating strong service and food quality across the platform.
- Consistently high ratings support customer trust and repeat usage.

## Conclusion
- Swiggy’s business performance is driven by urban demand, mid-priced meals, strong restaurant brands, and high customer satisfaction.
- Growth opportunities exist in expanding high-performing cities, promoting well-rated niche cuisines, and optimizing recommendation-driven ordering.
